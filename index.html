Dominique Villasenor

Hello! My name is Dominique Villasenor and I am a recent graduate from the University of California, Riverside. I am 24 years old. I was born and raised in Stockton, CA. I was a delegate for ASUCR’s Student Voice Committee and the president of the California Health Professional Student Alliance Chapter at UCR. As a first generation transfer student and a neuroscience major, I am interested in understanding the biological mechanisms of brain diseases and how it affects low income individual’s ability to access healthcare. I am a strong advocate for a universal healthcare system and aim to change our policy in healthcare. I plan to pursue a career in neuroscience and public policy by applying to a graduate program.

Fun Fact: I have always had a passion for swimming and the first job that I ever had was lifeguarding. I continued this passion by working as a lifeguard during my time as a UCR student.
